About Me

A hands-on CRM professional
with a story to tell

I build CRM systems from scratch — not just operate them. That distinction shapes everything I do.

I am a CRM Manager and Consultant with over a decade of experience in lifecycle marketing, automation and martech strategy. My career has spanned diverse industries — from iGaming to sports blockchain — where I have built and owned CRM systems from the ground up, not just managed them.

What sets my approach apart is the combination of strategic thinking and technical execution. I work closely with tech, product and operations teams as a key stakeholder, building the automation flows, user journeys and data frameworks that drive results — and leading the teams that bring it all to life.

My background in Industrial Design taught me to think in systems and user flows — a perspective I bring into every CRM project I work on.

Outside of work, I am a passionate basketball player with nearly 20 years on the court — a sport that taught me discipline, teamwork and how to perform under pressure. I am naturally curious and enjoy exploring new ideas, technologies and ways of working. I believe the best professionals never stop learning, and that mindset shapes everything I do.
